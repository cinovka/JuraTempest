Who is Rimuru ?

Rimuru Tempest is the main protagonist of "That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime" (Tensei Shitara Slime Datta Ken). Originally a human named Satoru Mikami, he is murdered and reincarnated in a fantasy world as a slime with unique abilities.

After being named by the Storm Dragon Veldora, Rimuru gains immense power and eventually becomes the founder and ruler of the Jura Tempest Federation, a nation that strives for harmony between monsters and humans.

Despite his small and unassuming form, Rimuru possesses incredible powers, a sharp intellect, and a compassionate heart that earns him the loyalty of countless followers.

Notable Abilities

Predator

Allows Rimuru to devour anything and analyze, replicate, or assimilate its properties.

Shapeshifter

Allows Rimuru to take on various forms, including human form, while retaining slime properties.

Great Sage

An advanced super-computation skill that provides analysis, advice, and precise control of abilities.

Magicule Control

Rimuru can efficiently control maglcules to perform powerful magic and skills.

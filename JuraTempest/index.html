About Rimuru

Rimuru Tempest is the main protagonist of "That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime." Originally a human named Satoru Mikami, he is reborn as a powerful slime in a fantasy world after dying protecting his colleague.

He quickly becomes one of the most powerful beings in the world, founding the Jura Tempest Federation and taking on the title of Demon Lord to protect his friends and subjects.

Abilities & Skills

Predator

Allows Rimuru to absorb and analyze anything, acquiring its abilities and properties.

Great Sage

An advanced cognitive processing skill that provides analysis, advice, and predictions.

Shape Shifter

Enables Rimuru to assume any form, humanoid or otherwise, that he has analyzed.

Magicule Breeder Reactor

Provides essentially infinite magicule energy for spells and abilities.
